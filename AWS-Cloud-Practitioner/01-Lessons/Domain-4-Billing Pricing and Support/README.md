# Domain 4: Billing, Pricing, and Support

## What this domain covers

Domain 4 is the smallest domain on the Cloud Practitioner exam at **12% (~8 questions)**. It covers how AWS pricing works, how to monitor and manage costs, and where to find help and support.

## Key terms

**Pay-as-you-go** — You pay only for what you use, with no upfront commitment or minimum fees. Stop using a service and charges stop.

**AWS Free Tier** — AWS provides certain services free of charge, either for 12 months after account creation, indefinitely (Always Free), or as short-term trials.

**Reserved Instance** — A commitment to use a service for 1 or 3 years in exchange for a significantly reduced price compared to on-demand.

**Savings Plans** — A flexible commitment to a certain amount of compute spend per hour (in dollars), in exchange for lower prices across EC2, Lambda, and Fargate.

**Consolidated billing** — A feature of AWS Organizations that combines billing across multiple AWS accounts into a single invoice, enabling volume discounts.

**Cost allocation tags** — Key-value labels attached to resources that appear in billing reports, allowing you to track costs by department, project, or environment.

**Technical Account Manager (TAM)** — A dedicated AWS technical contact included with Enterprise Support plans.

**Trusted Advisor** — An automated service that scans your AWS account and provides recommendations across cost, security, performance, and fault tolerance.
